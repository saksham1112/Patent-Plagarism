About PatentCheck

Revolutionizing patent analysis through artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies.

Our Story

Born from a 48-hour hackathon sprint, PatentCheck started as a rebellion against the hidden epidemic of patent plagiarism. Frustrated by innovators losing credit—and billions—to copied ideas, our team of engineers, attorneys, and AI specialists set out to build a tool that could detect stolen IP in seconds, not weeks. The spark? A Stanford researcher’s story of her algorithm being replicated verbatim in a foreign patent. Existing tools missed it—they couldn’t parse technical jargon or spot paraphrased claims. So we hacked together a prototype combining transformer-based NLP to dissect claims, semantic similarity models to flag overlaps, and a graph database mapping citation loopholes. By hour 36, our MVP exposed three plagiarized patents in real time. Now, we’re scaling the solution to empower startups and legal teams to fight back. Unlike legacy systems that only search keywords, PatentCheck’s AI mimics how attorneys analyze novelty, tracing even rewritten or fragmented infringement. Every line of code is built to democratize protection—because innovation shouldn’t be stolen with a thesaurus.

Our Mission

To empower innovators and protect intellectual property through advanced AI-driven patent analysis, making the patent system more transparent and accessible for everyone.

We believe that by detecting plagiarism and infringement more effectively, we can foster genuine innovation and reduce costly legal disputes in the patent ecosystem.
